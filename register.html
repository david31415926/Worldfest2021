Wednesday, April 21, 2021

4:00-9:00 PM

Location: Online

Cost: Free!

Filled with the vibrancy of our world’s myriad of cultures, WorldFest is a captivating celebration of the diversity displayed by the students and staff of Centennial High School. The event features unique opportunities to enjoy ethnic cuisine, dancing, activities, and artwork, all of which are produced and coordinated by the students themselves. Participants can gain appreciation for the many aspects of unfamiliar cultures by completing a scavenger hunt or watching the stunning WorldFest Talent Show. Mark your calendars, Centennial’s WorldFest is not to be missed!
