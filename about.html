Worldfest 2021

Due to current circumstances, Worldfest 2021 will be held virtually through Google Meet on Thursday, April 22! We are starting at 3:45p.m. ET and ending at 8:00p.m. ET., with various workshops and shows attendees can enjoy! This is a great opportunity to connect with friends while learning about and appreciating other cultures. Join us online for cooking shows, dance workshops, tutorials, quizzes, and more!


Worldfest 2021 will be divided into eight 30 minute long blocks, during which attendees can choose and join workshops for countries that they are interested in learning more about. Please refer to the Google Meet codes below in order to join our workshops! Note that attendees must be using their HCPSS accounts to join the meets.
